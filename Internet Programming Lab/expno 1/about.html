Sathyabama University, Chennai Sathyabama University is a Private College and it was established in 1987 and accredited by UGC, AICTE. Sathyabama University offers 89 courses in Arts and Humanities, Medical, Hotel Management, Pharmacy, Engineering streams. Popular courses are B.Tech, BSc, BDS, BBA, MA. Chennai (Tamil Nadu) Approved by : UGC, AICTE

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology (SIST), formerly Sathyabama University, is a Christian higher education deemed to be university institute, situated at Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India]. It was founded in 1987 as Sathyabama Engineering College by Jeppiaar and received its deemed to be university status in 2001.
